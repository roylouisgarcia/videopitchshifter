2 - Want to delete the last N seconds of a video and keep the rest. (Manually enter duration)


ffmpeg -ss 00:00:00 -to 01:32:00 -i input.mp4 -c copy output.mp4


Change from mp4 to mov

ffmpeg -i input_file.mp4 -f mov output_file.mov

Extracting the sounds (mp3) from video (mkv):

ffmpeg -i video.mkv -map 0:a:0 -b:a 320k sound.mp3

Combine back audio to original video (replacing original audio)

ffmpeg —i video.mkv —i lowered_sound.wav —c:v copy —c:a aac —map O:v:O —map 1:a:O video_lower.mp4
